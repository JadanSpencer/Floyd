About Us

Here at Floyds Distributors Ltd, we are committed to providing high-quality, sustainable packaging materials for businesses of all sizes. Our range includes everything from biodegradable bags and eco-friendly boxes to custom packaging solutions designed to meet your specific needs.

Founded in 2010, our mission has always been to deliver innovative and sustainable packaging materials while ensuring top-notch customer service and competitive pricing.

Our Mission

To lead the packaging industry by providing sustainable, innovative, and high-quality packaging materials that meet the needs of our customers while contributing to a greener future.
